## providing liquidity

want to earn fees instead of just holding? provide liquidity to the MYCEL/DAIMON pool:

1. go to clanker.exchange or aerodrome
2. navigate to "pools" or "liquidity"
3. find MYCEL/DAIMON pair
4. deposit equal value of both tokens
5. earn 0.3% on all trades through the pool

**risks:** impermanent loss if prices diverge significantly. only provide what you can afford to have locked.
